Series

Series Quarta A, Correspondence

  1. \(\LaTeX class\) the same for all volumes
    • Manuscripts can be submitted in various formats
      • Markdown (Quarto)
      • LaTeX Overleaf
  2. Uniform font size, title page and page layout for all volumes
  3. Hybrid publication by different media titles, page identical content
    • digital (BEG, Uni Basel, UB), ISSN
    • print (Publisher to be negotiated), ISBN
    • computational book, ISBN
  4. New volumes increase the Series IVA counter
    • E.g. Euler-Condorcet becomes IVA 9
    • Followed e.g. Euler-alia, IVA 10
  5. Only internal links, no external links
